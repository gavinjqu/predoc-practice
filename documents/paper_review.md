    1. The scan - Abstract, Intro, Conclusion and Tables
    2. Institutional context and data
    3. Robustness check - do results change after adding control or sample changes?

## 5. Identify the "Friction"
* In Labor and Health, there is always a "friction"
* Something that stops the market from being perfect (e.g., information asymmetry, travel costs, or "health type" frailty).
